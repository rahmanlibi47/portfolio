About me

My vision of coding

I had a different view about programming ever since I started seeing the codes. I could see the meaning and the outcome of the line of codes we were taught in the school days. I realized it was not just a bunch of gibberish lines to get us graduate from the school, but a meaningful language that was not as hard as it looked.

My path to programming

I could say I entered into programming as my passion, with the help of a couple of games that I loved playing in my school days. My passion for the games made me eager enough to dig up deeper into the game files, how the game worked coordinating sound, graphics and interaction from input devices, and I wanted to see if I could change gameplay like I wanted it to be.
